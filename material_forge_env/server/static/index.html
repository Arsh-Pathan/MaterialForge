Benchmark Specifications

MaterialForge is a reinforcement learning benchmark focused on inverse materials discovery. Agents are challenged to design an 8x8 atomic lattice that reproduces specific macro-properties.

Scoring & Reward Formula

The total reward is calculated after every operation using a complex scientific rubric:

R = 0.50 * match_score + 0.25 * packing_stability + 0.15 * symmetry + 0.10 * phase_bonus - cost_pen
  • Match Score: Proximity to the target property levels.
  • Packing Stability: Rewarded for consistent atomic neighbors.
  • Phase Bonus: Extra reward for reaching Crystalline or Polycrystalline states.
